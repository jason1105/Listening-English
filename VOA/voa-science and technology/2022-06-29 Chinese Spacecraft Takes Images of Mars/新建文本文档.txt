Chinese Spacecraft Takes Images of Mars
A Chinese spacecraft has taken images covering all of Mars after circling the planet more than 1,300 times since early last year, state media reported this week.
China's Tianwen-1, a spacecraft without people on it, reached the Red Planet in February 2021. 
It was the country's first mission there. A robotic rover has since been sent to the surface, while an orbiter studied the planet from space.
Among the images taken from space were China's first photographs of the Martian south pole. 
The south pole is where almost all of the planet's water resources are locked.
In 2018, an orbiter operated by the European Space Agency discovered water under the ice of the planet's south pole. 
Finding water below the surface is important for studying the planet's ability to provide for life. 
Such a finding could also be helpful for any human exploration there.
Other Tianwen-1 images include photographs of the Valles Marineris, a 4,000-kilometer long canyon, and an area in the north of Mars called Arabia Terra. 
It has many impact craters – holes in the ground caused by meteorites or other objects.
Tianwen-1 also sent back detailed images of the edge of the large Maunder crater, as well as a view of the 18,000-meter Ascraeus Mons, a large volcano first detected by NASA's Mariner 9 spacecraft more than 50 years ago.

中国航天器拍摄了火星照片
据官方媒体本周报道，自去年年初以来，中国一艘宇宙飞船在环绕火星1300多次后，拍摄了覆盖整个火星的照片。
中国的天文一号无人飞船于2021年2月抵达火星。
这是该国第一次在那里执行任务。此后，一辆机器人漫游者被送往火星表面，同时一架轨道飞行器从太空研究这颗行星。
在这些从太空拍摄的照片中，有中国首次拍摄的火星南极的照片。
南极几乎是火星上所有的水资源都被锁定的地方。
2018年，欧洲航天局的一颗人造卫星在火星南极冰层下发现了水。
寻找地表以下的水对于研究地球是否有能力提供生命是很重要的。
这样的发现对人类在那里的探索也有帮助。
“天文一号”的其他照片包括4000公里长的“水手谷”(vales Marineris)和火星北部一个名为“阿拉伯地”(Arabia Terra)的地区的照片。
它有许多陨石坑——由陨石或其他物体在地面上造成的洞。
“天文一号”还发回了大型蒙德火山口边缘的详细图像，以及1.8万米高的阿斯克劳斯蒙斯火山的照片，这是美国宇航局的“水手9号”航天器在50多年前首次探测到的一座大型火山。
